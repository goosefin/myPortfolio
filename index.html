About Me

My name is Agustina Fin but I go by Goose. I began my career as a small business owner working in the pet service industry. Like any company in todays day and age I needed a website and this is where my interest in web development began. I spent a lot of time creating a site that would reflect my passion for my business but quickly realized that the platform I was using limited my creativity.

After dabbling with youtube tutorials I signed up for a Udemy course to expand on my newly aquired skills and I quickly realized how amazing bringing my ideas from a concept to a functional application was. Shortly after I signed up for a software engineering bootcamp and that leads me to today!

I've now built out muliple projects with a variety of languages and continue learning all there is to this ever expanding industry
